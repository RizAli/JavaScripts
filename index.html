A review of different parts of speech

  1. A noun is the name of a person, place or a thing. Examples include umbrella, sidewalk and car.
  2. An adjective describes someone or something. Examples include creative, red, ugly and short.
  3. A verb is an action word. Examples include run, jump, swim, fly.
  4. An adverb tells how something is done. Examples include greedily, rapidly and carefully.
